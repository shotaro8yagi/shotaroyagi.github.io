Practice Designer + Sociologist

Hello! I’m a Practice Designer working as a project manager with COPILOT Inc. Tokyo and a PhD Candidate at the University of Tokyo. I also give lectures, talks, and consult on project management.

I’m currently based in Glasgow, UK.

Research

I study project management from sociological perspectives including practice theory, new pragmatism, social systems theory, network theory, and ontology. I do this not only theoretically, but also empirically through ethnography and statistical data analysis.

Interests

Work

I facilitate people to collaborate better —creating practices as we practice. Projects are not just containers for work; they are living dyanamics of people, artifacts, and environments.

I often engage in new product and business development, workshop design, organizational development, and delivering event and fellowship experiences.

Previous clients: SKword, Lion Corporation, The Institute of Science Tokyo, Google News Lab, NTT West, Toyota Connected, Eisenhower Fellowship (US).
